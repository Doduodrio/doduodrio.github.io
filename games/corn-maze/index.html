PLAY CORN MAZE

Warning: This game has sound


It's a cool autumn day! Putting on your straw hat, you decide to visit the corn maze. I hear it's lovely this time of year...


Controls:

[ARROW] keys to walk around.

[SPACE] key to interact with NPCs and progress the text. (Click the game window first)


Hint: Count all of the birds in the maze! Something special awaits you at the end of the maze if you do... (The birds in the start and end rooms don't count.)
